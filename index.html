Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about learning with Nubian

How long does it take to become conversational in Kinyarwanda?

With consistent practice of 20 minutes daily, most learners reach basic conversational fluency in 3-4 months. Our structured approach helps you progress faster by focusing on practical, everyday language first.

Do I need any prior language learning experience?

Not at all! Nubian is designed for complete beginners. Our step-by-step approach makes learning accessible for everyone, regardless of prior language learning experience.

What makes Nubian different from other language apps?

Nubian stands out through its cultural immersion approach, native speaker audio, and focus on real-world conversations. Unlike other apps, we integrate cultural context deeply into the learning process, helping you understand not just the words, but when and how to use them appropriately.

Will I be able to speak with native Kinyarwanda speakers?

Yes! Our dialogue-focused approach ensures you're learning natural, everyday Kinyarwanda as it's actually spoken. By completing the intermediate level, you'll be able to have comfortable conversations with native speakers on common topics.

What devices does Nubian work on?

Nubian works on iOS and Android smartphones and tablets. We also offer a web version that syncs your progress across all your devices, so you can learn anytime, anywhere.

What other African languages are coming to Nubian?

We're currently developing courses for Swahili, Yoruba, Amharic, and Zulu. Join our email list to be the first to know when new languages are released!
